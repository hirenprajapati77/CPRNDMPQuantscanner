# NDMP OS v6.0 – Quantitative Research Operating System

NDMP OS v6.0 is an institutional quantitative research and execution operating system designed to discover, validate, and exploit directional momentum edges in Indian NSE Futures & Options (F&O) markets.

---

## 🏗️ Repository Architecture

The platform is structured into 4 decoupled sub-modules:

* `ndmp-core/`: Production Execution Engine, Broker APIs, and Market Replay Engine.
* `ndmp-research/`: Feature Store (`BaseFeature` plugins), Market Regime Models, and `feature.yaml` manifests.
* `ndmp-validation/`: 5 Governance Promotion Gates, CPCV, Deflated Sharpe Ratio (DSR), and PBO Auditing.
* `ndmp-knowledge/`: Institutional Knowledge Bank holding Architecture Decision Records (ADRs), Experiment Logs, and Decision Journals.
